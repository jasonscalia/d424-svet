About SVET

Simple Vacation and Excursion Tracker (SVET) helps you plan, organize, and track your travel experiences. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a month-long adventure, SVET provides all the tools you need.

Key Features

  • Create and manage trip itineraries
  • Link excursions to trips
  • Search through your personal database of destinations and excursions
  • Share travel plans with others
  • Offline access to your information
  • Vendor agnostic

Installation

  1. Download the APK file using the button above
  2. Go to Settings > Security on your Android device
  3. Enable "Unknown Sources" or "Install unknown apps"
  4. Open your file manager and find the downloaded APK
  5. Tap the APK file to install
  6. Follow the on-screen prompts
  7. Launch SVET from your app drawer

Requirements

  • Android 16.0 or higher
  • 15 MB free storage space
  • Internet connection for initial setup, but not required for use of mobile application
